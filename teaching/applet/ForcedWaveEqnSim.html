Problem B: Forced Wave Equation
u_tt = u_xx + sin(2πx)
u(x,t) Current
v(x) Steady State
F(x) Force
Speed: 1.0x
t = 0.00s
Analysis: The string oscillates entirely on one side of the initial position (u=0). The constant force sin(2πx) pushes it towards the red steady-state line. The string "overshoots" the steady state, reaches a maximum deformation of 2*v(x), and then swings back to the flat initial position.
